## Leyenda de regiones

- рҹҺ© **London / Cockney & MLE** (`LDN`)
- рҹҸҙу Ғ§у Ғўу Ғіу ҒЈу Ғҙу Ғҝ **Scotland** (`SCO`)
- рҹҸҙу Ғ§у Ғўу Ғ·у Ғ¬у Ғіу Ғҝ **Wales** (`WAL`)
- вҳҳпёҸ **Northern Ireland** (`NI`)
- вҡ“ **Liverpool (Scouse)** (`LIV`)
- рҹҢү **Newcastle (Geordie)** (`NCL`)
- рҹҗқ **Manchester (Mancunian)** (`MAN`)
- рҹ”§ **Birmingham (Brummie)** (`BIR`)
- рҹҗ‘ **Yorkshire** (`YOR`)
- рҹӨі **Gen-Z UK (nationwide)** (`WC`)

> Nota: el MLE (Multicultural London English) y el slang Gen-Z se difunden por todo el paГӯs vГӯa mГәsica (grime/drill) y redes.
